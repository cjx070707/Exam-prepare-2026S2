**Question 1.** *(Why CNN fails on graphs ★★★)* A team applies a standard CNN to molecular graphs, where atoms are nodes with feature vectors and bonds are edges. The model performs poorly. What is the fundamental structural mismatch?

- [ ] A. Graph nodes lack spatial coordinates, so the CNN's position-sensitive filters cannot extract atom features that have no fixed 2D layout
- [ ] B. Graphs have variable neighbour counts per node and no canonical ordering of neighbours, making fixed-size kernels with positionally-indexed weights unsuitable
- [ ] C. CNNs require inputs with uniform channel depth across positions, but molecular graphs have nodes with different numbers of bonds, producing inconsistent feature dimensions
- [ ] D. The graph adjacency matrix is sparse, and standard dense convolution wastes computation on zero entries that a CNN cannot efficiently ignore

> [!note]- Answer
> **B**
>
> CNN 的卷积核是固定大小的，且对位置敏感（左边邻居和右边邻居权重不同）。图中每个节点的邻居数量可变（度不固定），且邻居无空间顺序，无法定义"上/下/左/右"——这使得固定大小、位置敏感的卷积核根本无法应用。
> - **A 错**：坐标缺失是表现，根本原因是邻居数可变 + 无空间顺序
> - **C 错**：特征维度不一致不是 CNN 的根本问题
> - **D 错**：稀疏性是计算效率问题，不是结构上的根本不匹配

**Question 2.** *(对称归一化 vs 行归一化 ★★★)* A GCN uses row normalisation ($\hat{D}^{-1}\hat{A}$). A reviewer argues for Kipf's symmetric normalisation ($\hat{D}^{-\frac{1}{2}}\hat{A}\hat{D}^{-\frac{1}{2}}$) instead. What is the key mechanistic difference?

- [ ] A. Row normalisation divides each neighbour's contribution by the receiving node's degree only; symmetric normalisation additionally scales by the sending node's degree
- [ ] B. Row normalisation requires the graph to be undirected to guarantee row sums equal 1; symmetric normalisation works on both directed and undirected graphs
- [ ] C. Symmetric normalisation produces row and column sums that both equal 1, while row normalisation guarantees only that row sums equal 1
- [ ] D. Row normalisation treats all neighbours as equal contributors; symmetric normalisation applies learned attention weights to down-weight high-degree neighbours

> [!note]- Answer
> **A**
>
> 行归一化：节点 i 的更新 = $(1/\hat{d}_i) \sum_j \hat{A}_{ij} h_j$，只除以**接收**节点 i 的度。
>
> 对称归一化：权重 = $\hat{A}_{ij}/\sqrt{\hat{d}_i \cdot \hat{d}_j}$，同时除以发送节点 j 和接收节点 i 的度——高度发送方贡献更少，高度接收方接收也被稀释，双向公平。
> - **B 错**：行归一化和无向图的关系描述有误
> - **C 错**：对称归一化不保证行列和均为 1（自环改变了度数）
> - **D 错**：对称归一化是固定的度数加权，不是可学习的 attention

**Question 3.** *(Spectral GCN v1.0 vs v2.0 ★★)* A Spectral GCN v1.0 trains a diagonal filter $g_\theta(\Lambda)$ with one parameter per eigenvalue on a graph with n=10,000 nodes. A v2.0 model uses $\sum_{j=0}^{K} \alpha_j \Lambda^j$ with K=5. What are the two primary advantages of v2.0 over v1.0?

- [ ] A. v2.0 reduces trainable parameters from n to K, and operates directly on the Laplacian L without requiring eigendecomposition
- [ ] B. v2.0 reduces trainable parameters from n to K, and guarantees higher node classification accuracy through polynomial filter expressiveness
- [ ] C. v2.0 eliminates eigendecomposition and supports directed graphs, whereas v1.0 requires a symmetric Laplacian
- [ ] D. v2.0 reduces per-layer computation and extends the receptive field to K hops by stacking K graph convolutional layers

> [!note]- Answer
> **A**
>
> v1.0 问题：(1) #param = n（随节点数增长）；(2) 每次前向传播需要完整特征分解 O(N³)。
>
> v2.0 改进：$g_\theta(\Lambda) = \sum \alpha_j \Lambda^j$，只有 K 个参数；$Ug_\theta(\Lambda)U^T = \sum \alpha_j L^j$，直接作用于 Laplacian，无需特征分解。K 同时表示感受野（L^j = j 跳邻居）。
> - **B 错**：v2.0 不保证更高精度，准确率不是对比维度
> - **C 错**：有向图支持不是 PDF 中讨论的 v2.0 优势
> - **D 错**：K 是多项式阶数（感受野），不是堆叠层数

**Question 4.** *(任务输出公式 ★★)* A GCN produces node embeddings $z_n \in \mathbb{R}^d$ for a citation network. The team needs to (i) classify each paper's research area and (ii) predict whether two unconnected papers should be linked. Which output formulas correspond to these two tasks?

- [ ] A. (i) $\text{softmax}(z_n)$ per node；(ii) $\sigma(z_i^T z_j)$ per candidate pair
- [ ] B. (i) $\text{softmax}(\sum_n z_n)$；(ii) $\sigma(\|z_i - z_j\|_2)$ per candidate pair
- [ ] C. (i) $\text{softmax}(z_n)$ per node；(ii) $\text{softmax}(z_i^T z_j)$ per candidate pair
- [ ] D. (i) $\sigma(Wz_n)$ per node；(ii) $\sigma(z_i^T z_j)$ per candidate pair

> [!note]- Answer
> **A**
>
> (i) 节点分类：每个节点独立分类 → $\text{softmax}(z_n)$
> (ii) 链接预测：两节点是否相连是二分类 → $\sigma(z_i^T z_j)$（内积衡量相似度，sigmoid 输出概率）
> - **B 错**：$\text{softmax}(\sum_n z_n)$ 是图级别分类（整张图一个标签），不是节点级
> - **C 错**：链接预测是二分类，用 sigmoid 不是 softmax
> - **D 错**：$\sigma(Wz_n)$ 是合理变体，但 PDF 给出的标准公式是 $\text{softmax}(z_n)$

**Question 5.** *(Kipf GCN 推导 ★★★)* A graph has N nodes, adjacency matrix A, and node feature matrix $X \in \mathbb{R}^{N \times d}$. A GCN layer is being designed. **(6 Marks)**

(a) The naive formulation $\sigma(AH^l W^l)$ has two problems. State each problem and its fix. **(2 Marks)**

(b) Write Kipf's full normalised GCN formula. Define each symbol. Explain why symmetric normalisation is preferred over row normalisation $\hat{D}^{-1}\hat{A}$. **(3 Marks)**

(c) In a two-layer Kipf GCN, what is the effective receptive field of each node? Explain why. **(1 Mark)**

> [!note]- Answer
> **(a)**
> **问题 1**：乘以 A 只聚合邻居特征，不包含节点自身 → 修复：加自环，$\hat{A} = A + I_N$
>
> **问题 2**：A 未归一化，高度节点的特征数值尺度远大于低度节点，聚合后特征值不稳定 → 修复：用归一化矩阵代替 $\hat{A}$
>
> **(b)**
> $$H^{(l+1)} = \sigma\!\left(\hat{D}^{-\frac{1}{2}}\hat{A}\hat{D}^{-\frac{1}{2}} H^{(l)} W^{(l)}\right)$$
>
> - $\hat{A} = A + I_N$（含自环的邻接矩阵）
> - $\hat{D}$：$\hat{A}$ 的对角度矩阵，$\hat{D}_{ii} = \sum_j \hat{A}_{ij}$
> - $H^{(l)}$：第 $l$ 层节点表示矩阵，$H^{(0)} = X$；$W^{(l)}$：可学习权重矩阵
>
> 对称归一化的优势：行归一化 $\hat{D}^{-1}\hat{A}$ 只除以接收节点 i 的度，高度发送方的贡献未被压缩，仍会主导聚合。对称归一化权重 = $\hat{A}_{ij}/\sqrt{\hat{d}_i \hat{d}_j}$，同时压缩发送方和接收方的度，统计上更稳定。
>
> **(c)**
> 2 层 GCN 的有效感受野 = **2 跳邻居**。每层聚合一跳：第 1 层后节点包含 1 跳邻居信息；第 2 层将邻居的 1 跳聚合再聚合，等价于当前节点看到 2 跳以内所有节点的原始特征。

**Question 6.** *(Spatial vs Spectral 对比 ★★)* A team is choosing between Spatial GCN (Kipf) and Spectral GCN for a social network with 10 million nodes, where the model must also generalise to newly-joined users not seen during training. **(4 Marks)**

(a) Compare the scalability of Spectral v1.0, Spectral v2.0, and Spatial GCN. Which is most practical for 10 million nodes and why? **(2 Marks)**

(b) Why does Spectral GCN v1.0 struggle to generalise to new, unseen nodes (inductive setting), while Spatial GCN handles this naturally? **(2 Marks)**

> [!note]- Answer
> **(a)**
> - **Spectral v1.0**：需完整特征分解 $L = U\Lambda U^T$，复杂度 O(N³)，#param = N。1000 万节点完全不可行。
> - **Spectral v2.0**：直接用 $L^j$，无需特征分解，#param = K（小）。稀疏图优化后复杂度 O(KE)，可扩展但仍依赖全图 Laplacian。
> - **Spatial GCN（Kipf）**：线性复杂度 O(E)，权重矩阵大小与 N 无关，天然支持 mini-batch 和分布式。
>
> **最适合大规模：Spatial GCN（Kipf）**，唯一具有线性复杂度且不依赖全图操作。
>
> **(b)**
> Spectral v1.0 的滤波器 $g_\theta(\Lambda)$ 是针对当前图的特征值 $\Lambda$ 定义的——特征向量 $U$ 取决于整体图结构。新图（加入新用户）有不同的拉普拉斯矩阵，特征分解结果不同，训练好的 $\theta$ 对新图的 $\Lambda'$ 失去意义，无法迁移。这是 transductive 限制。
>
> Spatial GCN 的权重 $W^{(l)}$ 作用于节点特征向量本身，与图拓扑无关——只要新节点有特征向量和边连接，就能直接前向传播，天然支持 inductive 设置。
