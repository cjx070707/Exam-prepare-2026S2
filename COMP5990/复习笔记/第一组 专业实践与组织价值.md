## Part 1 — IT Professional Practice（W1）

> [!info] 问题链
> 学生常以为「系统能跑就行」→ 真实环境里需求不全、多方利益冲突 → 要从 **can we build it?** 转到 **should we do this?** → 靠沟通、问责、professional judgement 落地

### IT professional practice 是什么 `★★★`

> [!tip] 情景题：客户抱怨沟通晚、机密处理随意——这属于哪类问题？

**中文**：IT professional practice 讲的是 **真实组织里 IT 工作如何被完成**，不只是技术本身。包括：

- 在需求、风险、信息 **不完整** 时做决策
- 在 **strategy / cost / time / quality** 约束下平衡技术选择
- 向技术与非技术 **stakeholders** 沟通风险、权衡与不确定性
- 在交付压力与变更下 **ethical、responsible** 行事
- 对决策、结果与 **professional judgement** 负责

课件强调：重点往往在 **技术方案提出之后** 发生什么（治理、对齐、问责），而不只是写代码。

**Term**

| English | 中文 |
|---------|------|
| IT professional practice | IT 专业实践 |
| incomplete requirements / information | 需求或信息不完整 |
| trade-offs | 权衡 |
| delivery pressure | 交付压力 |
| professional judgement | 专业判断 |
| accountability | 问责、承担责任 |

**Useful phrase**

- *IT professional practice is about how IT work is carried out in **real organisations**, not just the technology itself.*
- *It involves **balancing technical choices** with organisational strategy, cost, time, and quality constraints.*
- *Success depends on how well technology **fits within organisational constraints and decision-making structures**.*

<span style="color: #9b59b6">💡 **白话**：会写代码是入场券；这门课考的是「在烂信息、多老板、有限预算里怎么负责任地做决定」。</span>

<span style="color: #e67e22">*技术可行之后，还要回答「该不该做、谁承担后果」——见下一节。*</span>

---

### When technical excellence is not enough `★★`

**中文**：许多从业者默认：系统能跑、设计合理、代码正确 → 就该成功。课件认为 **technical competence is essential but not sufficient**。

**Term**：technical competence / technical excellence

**Useful phrase**

- *Technical competence is essential, but it is **not sufficient on its own**.*

---

### IT systems do not exist in isolation `★★`

**中文**：真实系统处在组织现实中：

- 层级、权威、影响力塑造决策
- 预算与时间常被谈判、约束、固定
- 政策、法规、法律限制可行方案
- 团队与 stakeholders 目标与激励不同

成功不只取决于 build 得好不好，还取决于技术是否 **fit organisational constraints**。

**Term**：organisational constraints / decision-making structures

**Useful phrase**

- *Success is determined not only by how well a system is built, but by how well technology **fits within organisational constraints**.*

---

### The nature of real IT work `★★★`

**中文**：真实 IT 工作很少一开始就清晰，特征包括：

- 需求随理解深入而 **evolve**
- Stakeholders 看重不同成果、用不同标准衡量成功
- 常在信息不全时就要 **commit to decisions**

**竞争优先级示例**（课件）：

| 视角 | 倾向 |
|------|------|
| Business | speed, market impact |
| IT | stability, maintainability, scalability |
| Security | risk reduction, compliance |

Professional practice = 识别这些冲突，把 **trade-offs explicit**，在多方张力下 **navigate responsibly**，而不是只优化单一视角。

**Term**：competing priorities / make trade-offs explicit / navigate tensions responsibly

**Useful phrase**

- *Requirements **evolve** as understanding improves.*
- *Professional practice is about making **trade-offs explicit** rather than optimising for only one perspective.*

---

### Technical feasibility vs professional judgement `★★★`

> [!tip] 面试/案例：「我们能做云迁移」——还要问什么？

| | Technical questions | Professional questions |
|--|---------------------|------------------------|
| 焦点 | 可行性 | 后果与责任 |
| 例子 | Can this be built? Which architecture? | Should we proceed now or delay? What risks are we accepting? Who is affected? |

核心转变：从 **“Can we do this?”** → **“Should we do this under these conditions?”**

**Term**：technical feasibility / professional judgement / consequences

**Useful phrase**

- *IT professional practice moves from **“can we do this?”** to **“should we do this under these conditions?”***
- *Professional questions focus on **what risks we are accepting and on whose behalf**.*

---

### Communication, accountability, responsibility `★★★`

**中文**：许多问题升级是因为：

- 风险已知但 **poorly communicated**
- 假设 **remain implicit**
- 责任太分散，**no one owns it**

Professional practice 强调：

- 清楚表达 **uncertainty**
- **Escalate concerns early**
- 对 **decisions** 负责，而不只是完成任务

**Term**：escalate concerns / implicit assumptions / take responsibility for decisions

**Useful phrase**

- *Risks are known but **poorly communicated**.*
- *Responsibility is spread so widely that **no one owns it**.*
- *Professional practice emphasises **escalating concerns early**.*

---

### Professionalism in IT（Practice Q1） `★★★`

> [!tip] Practice Q1：定义 professionalism；举 accountability 行为；提组织举措。

**中文（答题逻辑）**

- **Professionalism**：以 ethical、responsible 方式运用技术专长，交付 reliable solutions，建立信任、保护声誉。
- **Accountability 行为**：按时交付并 own mistakes；诚实沟通进度；维护 client confidentiality。
- **组织举措**：mandatory onboarding（统一专业标准）；peer-feedback / mentoring。

**Term**：professionalism / ethical and responsible / reliable solutions / own mistakes / client confidentiality / peer-feedback / mentoring

**Useful phrase**（Practice + 课件）

- *IT professionalism means applying technical expertise **ethically and responsibly** to deliver **reliable solutions** that meet client needs.*
- *It builds **trust** and protects the organisation’s **reputation**.*
- *Examples include **meeting deadlines and owning mistakes**, and **maintaining client confidentiality**.*

<span style="color: #e67e22">*专业侧讲清后，W2 转向 IT 如何为组织创造可衡量的价值。*</span>

### 课件案例教训（可作 essay 论据） `★★`

| 案例 | Core issue（课件） | Lesson |
|------|-------------------|--------|
| **ASX** (2024–25) | 技术复杂，但挑战在 **governance, planning, risk management, accountability** | Technical solutions need **equally robust professional governance and organisational alignment** |
| **Victorian Govt digital** (2022–24) | 非技术不成熟，而是 **cross-agency governance, risk escalation, decision-making under public pressure** | **Professional judgement, coordination, accountability** as critical as technical capability |

**Useful phrase**

- *The challenges were **organisational**, including governance, planning, and accountability.*
- *Treat security, compliance, and governance as **design constraints, not afterthoughts**.*

## Part 2 — IT 在组织中的角色（W1）

### Role of IT: Enable / Support / Transform `★★`

**中文**：IT 的作用是 **enable, support, and transform** 个人、企业、政府如何存储、处理、沟通与访问信息；是现代运营与创新的 **backbone**。

**Term**：enable / support / transform / backbone of modern operations

**Useful phrase**

- *The role of IT is to **enable, support, and transform** the way organisations store, process, communicate, and access information.*

---

### Core roles of IT `★`

**中文（列举）**：沟通协作；数据管理与存储；流程自动化；系统开发与维护；网络安全与风险管理；支持创新与数字化转型；技术支持与排障。

**Term**：process automation / digital transformation / cybersecurity and risk management

---

### Role of IT in organizations（价值表述） `★★`

**中文**：

- IT 通过改变 business functions and processes 的运作方式提供价值
- IT 通过提供 **new functions** 并 **enabling new business models** 提供价值

**Term**：business functions and processes / new business models

**Useful phrase**

- *IT provides value by **changing the way business functions and processes are carried out**.*
- *IT enables **new business models**.*

---

### Changing business landscape `★`

**中文**：重塑组织运营与竞争的四种力量：**business drivers, technology drivers, customer expectations, market trends**。

**Term**：business drivers / technology drivers / customer expectations / market trends

## Part 3 — 组织、结构与价值概念（W2）

> [!info] 问题链
> 先定义 organisation 与 value → 再看结构如何影响协作 → 最后用 IT investment 把资源变成 capabilities，产出 organisational value

### Organisation vs Business `★★`

| | **Organisation** | **Business** |
|--|------------------|--------------|
| 范围 | 广义：结构化群体为共同目标协作 | Organisation 的一种；**primary goal = profit** |
| 目的 | 不一定以盈利为导向 | 通过商品或服务盈利 |
| 例子 | Red Cross, NSW Health, USyd, sports club | Canva, Apple, local café |

**Organisation 五要素**（课件）：Purpose / People / Structure / System / Resources

**Term**：formal vs informal organisation / primary goal / mission or objective

**Useful phrase**

- *An organisation is a **structured group of people** working together to achieve specific goals.*
- *A business is a type of organisation whose **primary goal is to make a profit**.*

---

### Organisational structure 四种类型 `★★★`

> [!tip] 案例：BrightCare 从 functional 转 matrix——为什么？（W2 期末思考题）

**中文**：组织结构定义任务、责任、**authority** 如何分配；说明汇报关系、决策权、效率与沟通、如何管理成长与变革。

| 类型 | 特点 | Pros | Cons | Best for |
|------|------|------|------|----------|
| **Functional** | 按职能分部门（IT, HR, Finance） | 专业化、角色清晰、易按部门扩展 | 跨部门沟通弱、**silos**、灵活性差 | 大型、部门清晰（tech, banks） |
| **Matrix** | 向 **two managers** 汇报（职能 + 项目） | 跨项目用资源、团队协作、灵活 | **Dual authority** 易混乱、需强沟通 | 项目型、全球、咨询、R&D、IT services |
| **Flat** | 少或无中层 | 决策快、创新协作 | 角色易混、难扩展 | 初创、小团队 |
| **Hierarchical** | 传统自上而下、多层权威 | 汇报清晰、稳定、易管大团队 | 决策慢、抑制自主 | 政府、大型传统企业 |

**课件例子**：Microsoft（functional）、Google（matrix）、Valve（flat）、IBM（hierarchical）

**Term**：organisational structure / reporting lines / decision-making authority / silos / dual authority / chain of command

**Useful phrase**

- *A matrix structure means employees report to **two managers**—one for function and one for project.*
- *Functional structures can create **silos** and reduce flexibility across departments.*
- *A move to a matrix structure can **enable cross-departmental collaboration**.*（BrightCare 案例用语）

---

### Value vs Organisational value `★★★`

| | **Value** | **Organisational value** |
|--|-----------|---------------------------|
| 范围 | 广义，任何对象 | 仅组织通过运营与使命创造、交付、维持的价值 |
| 问题 | How does this benefit me or others? | How does the organisation create meaningful outcomes for **stakeholders**? |
| 利益相关方 | 常是个人/小规模 | customers, employees, shareholders, suppliers, community |
| 衡量 | 常主观 | KPIs, profit, satisfaction, reputation, innovation, social impact |
| 例子 | 一辆车有使用与转售价值 | Toyota 为客户、员工、股东等创造综合价值 |

**Tangible vs Intangible value**：金钱、产品 vs 信任、满意度

**Term**：tangible / intangible / stakeholders / meaningful outcomes

**Useful phrase**

- *Organisational value refers to value **created, delivered, and sustained** for stakeholders.*
- *It is often measured through metrics such as **KPIs, customer satisfaction, and brand reputation**.*

---

### IT investment / Resources vs Capabilities `★★★`

**IT investment（中文）**：把财务、人力、技术资源投入 IT 系统、工具或服务，以支持或改进运营、绩效或 **strategic goals**；可 **insourced** 或 **outsourced**。包括 hardware, software, data, people, processes, frameworks。

| | **Resources** | **Capabilities** |
|--|---------------|------------------|
| 含义 | 组织 **拥有** 什么 | 组织 **能做什么**（用资源执行行动达成结果） |
| 例子 | 物业、机器、硬件；知识、员工技能、政策 | 基于技能与技术执行某 course of action 的能力 |

**概念链**（课件）：Organisation → IT Investment → Value → **Organisational Value**

**Term**：IT investment / insourced / outsourced / strategic goals / resources / capabilities

**Useful phrase**

- *An IT investment is the allocation of resources into technology to support an organisation’s **strategic goals**.*
- *Resources refer to what an organisation **owns**; capabilities refer to what it **can do** using those resources.*

## Part 4 — IT 如何创造组织价值（W2）

### IT 创造 organisational value 的路径 `★★★`

> [!tip] Practice Q2a：IT 超越效率的两种价值——可对齐下面几条。

**中文**：IT 是创造、交付、维持价值的 **central / strategic enabler**（不只是 support function）。途径包括：

- **Operational efficiency** — automation, cloud, databases
- **Decision making** — data analytics, BI, AI
- **Innovation** — IoT, blockchain, agentic AI 等
- **Customer-centric strategies** — CRM, self-service
- **Scalability and flexibility** — cloud providers (AWS, GCP, Azure)

**Netflix vs Blockbuster（课件）**：Netflix 投资 streaming、推荐算法、云、内容分析 → customer-centric model、scalability、market disruption、**sustainable competitive advantage**；Blockbuster 未能适应数字化。

**Term**：strategic enabler / operational efficiency / data-driven decision-making / sustainable competitive advantage / customer-centric

**Useful phrase**

- *IT is not just a support function—it is a **strategic enabler** of innovation, efficiency, and competitiveness.*
- *IT enables **data-driven decision-making** and **innovation** beyond basic efficiency.*
- *Investments should create **sustainable competitive advantage**.*（Netflix 案例）

<span style="color: #e67e22">*单讲 IT 能做什么不够，还要和 business 两侧一一对齐。*</span>

---

### Aligning IT and Business（对齐框架） `★★★`

> [!tip] 大题常考：从 business goal 一路写到 IT capabilities 和 organisational value。

**两侧结构**（课件 diagram）：

| **Organisation side** | **IT investment side** |
|-----------------------|-------------------------|
| Business goals and objectives | IT strategy |
| Business strategy | IT operating model |
| Business model | IT capabilities |
| Business operating model | |
| Business capabilities | |

**关键概念（中文 + 考试常用英文）**

| 概念 | 要点 |
|------|------|
| **Business goal** | 组织想达成的方向（例：提高客户满意度） |
| **Business objective** | 可衡量的目标（例：12 个月内满意度升 X%） |
| **Business strategy** | 实现 goal 的高层计划：市场、定位、资源分配；**IT must be aligned** |
| **IT strategy** | 技术如何支持 business strategy；须 **in lockstep** with business strategy |
| **Business model** | 价值如何 create, deliver, capture；常联系 **Business Model Canvas (BMC)** |
| **Business operating model** | 组织如何运作：集中/分散、决策权、部门依赖 → 决定 IT governance |
| **IT operating model** | IT 如何组织与交付：service delivery, vendor management, agile 等 |
| **Business capabilities** | 业务能做什么（logistics, R&D, customer service…） |
| **IT capabilities** | IT 能交付什么（integration, cloud, analytics, cybersecurity…）→ **support and scale** business capabilities |

**QuickCart 例子**（课件串联用）：goal = ultra-fast delivery；objective = within 30 minutes；strategy = speed + real-time tracking + AI logistics；IT strategy = tech-first (AI, cloud)；capabilities 含 AI demand forecasting, route optimization 等。

**Term**：business goals and objectives / in lockstep / business model canvas / operating model / business capabilities / IT capabilities / digital transformation

**Useful phrase**

- *Value begins with a clear understanding of what the organisation wants to achieve.*
- *IT strategy must be developed **in lockstep with business strategy**.*
- *IT capabilities **support and scale** business capabilities.*
- *Use **enterprise-architecture mapping** and **joint IT–business planning sessions** to link projects to strategic objectives.*（Practice Q2b）

### Value stream orchestration `★★★`

> [!tip] W2 期末思考题直接问过；BrightCare 案例也强调 connect IT workflows with patient care。

**中文**：协调 business 与 IT，**持续交付价值**；同步 people, processes, technology，最大化对 stakeholders 的价值；保证技术投资产生 **real business outcomes**；从 idea 到 delivery 的价值流顺畅。

**Term**：value stream orchestration / continuous value / real business outcomes

**Useful phrase**

- *Value stream orchestration coordinates business and IT to **deliver continuous value**.*
- *It involves **synchronising people, processes, and technology** to maximise value for stakeholders.*
- *Orchestration ensures technology investments produce **real business outcomes**.*

---

### Best practices for IT investments `★★`

**中文（课件要点）**：

- 每项 IT 投资支持 overall business goals；**engage business leaders** in IT planning
- 为每项投资设 **measurable outcomes**（cost savings, efficiency, customer experience）
- 用 **KPIs** 跟踪 **ROI**；用 cost-benefit 或 **value-risk matrix** 排序项目
- 短期赢 + 长期价值；可扩展技术（cloud, APIs, modular platforms）；避免 **lock-in**
- 在低风险区试验新兴技术；平衡 innovation 与 practical business value

**Term**：measurable outcomes / value-risk matrix / lock-in / cost-benefit

**Useful phrase**

- *Ensure every IT investment **supports overall business goals**.*
- *Set **measurable outcomes** and use **KPIs to track ROI over time**.*
- *Track **ROI** or productivity using KPIs tied to **cost savings or customer satisfaction**.*（Practice Q2c）

## Part 5 — 案例与情景题素材

### BrightCare（W2 课件案例） `★★★`

**背景**：中型私立医疗；telehealth + 数字病历；系统老旧、部门 siloed、手工 reporting。

**计划**：云 HIS；聘 **CIO** 对齐 IT 与 vision；**functional → matrix**；明确 business goals（如 wait time ↓30%、digital record accuracy 95%）；analytics / real-time reporting；**value stream orchestration** 连接 IT 与 patient care。

**答题角度（课件问题）**

| 问 | 思路 |
|----|------|
| 两个 business goals + 与 organisational value | 如改善服务交付、患者满意度、降本 → 对应 stakeholder outcomes |
| 原结构 + 为何 matrix | 原 **functional**（易 silo）；matrix 促进 **cross-departmental collaboration** |
| CIO 角色与挑战 | 对齐 IT/business strategy；挑战：变革阻力、技能、治理、legacy systems |
| IT 投资是否创造价值 | 用 alignment、capabilities、orchestration、KPI/ROI 论证；也要提风险与 change management |

**Term**：Chief Information Officer (CIO) / health information system (HIS) / cross-departmental collaboration / legacy systems

**Useful phrase**

- *The CIO is responsible for **aligning IT strategy with the organisation’s vision**.*
- *Restructuring to a matrix model **enables cross-departmental collaboration**.*
- ***Value stream orchestration** connects IT workflows with patient care delivery.*

---
