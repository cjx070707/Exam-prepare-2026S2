## Part 1 — Project Management Essentials（W3）

### What is a project `★★`

> [!tip] 案例：开发新 App、上 CRM、云迁移——它们为什么是「项目」而不是日常运维？

**中文**

- **Project**：为创造 **unique** 的 product / service / result 而进行的 **temporary endeavour**
- 特征：**temporary**（有起止）、**unique**（非常规结果）、**progressive elaboration**（分步细化）

**Term**：temporary endeavour / unique product or result / progressive elaboration

**Useful phrase**

- *A project is a **temporary endeavour** undertaken to create a **unique** product, service, or result.*

---

### 不同角色关心什么 `★`

| Role | What they care about |
|------|---------------------|
| Executive Sponsor | strategic impact |
| Project Manager | time, cost, scope balance |
| Developer | clear requirements, technical feasibility |
| Operations | stability, security, maintainability |
| Customer | value for money |
| Regulator | compliance, risk |
| Society | privacy, ethical impact |

**Term**：executive sponsor / scope balance / technical feasibility / stakeholder（泛用）

---

### Success criteria & why projects fail `★★★`

**成功标准（completion）**

- **On-time delivery**
- **On-budget**
- **Meets scope & quality**
- **Stakeholder satisfaction**
- **Business value**（cost savings, efficiency, competitive advantage）

**失败原因（课件）**

- Poor scope definition（需求不清、频繁变更）
- Unrealistic timelines or budgets
- Weak stakeholder engagement
- Inadequate risk management
- Poor communication
- Resource issues（技能/技术不足）

**Term**：on-time delivery / on-budget / meets scope and quality / stakeholder satisfaction / business value / poor scope definition / inadequate risk management

**Useful phrase**

- *Success includes **meets scope and quality** and delivers measurable **business value**.*
- *Projects fail due to **unrealistic timelines or budgets** and **weak stakeholder engagement**.*

---

### Hidden root cause: professional breakdown `★★`

| Technical symptom | Professional failure |
|-------------------|----------------------|
| Scope creep | weak boundary management |
| Budget overrun | unrealistic executive pressure |
| User rejection | poor stakeholder engagement |
| Late defect discovery | communication gaps |

**Term**：scope creep / boundary management / communication gaps

**Useful phrase**

- *Many failures reflect **professional breakdown**, not only technical issues.*

---

### PM’s ethical dilemma & IT technician vs professional `★★`

**中文**：PM 夹在 executive expectations、budget、user needs、technical constraints、organisational politics 之间；须 **balance delivery with integrity**。

| Technician | Professional |
|------------|--------------|
| Executes tasks | Exercises judgement |
| Follows requirements | Challenges unclear requirements |
| Focuses on output | Focuses on impact |
| Solves bugs | Solves organisational problems |
| Thinks technically | Thinks strategically & ethically |

**Term**：balance delivery with integrity / exercises judgement / challenges unclear requirements

---

### Sydney Metro 案例（Agile 应用） `★`

**成功因素**：向运营方收集清晰需求；**phased delivery using Agile sprints**；持续 stakeholder engagement。  
**挑战**：硬件集成延误；供应链导致预算压力。  
**结果**：2023 全面运行；调度时间减少 35%；乘客实时信息提升满意度。

**Term**：phased delivery / Agile sprints / continuous stakeholder engagement

## Part 2 — PM Methodologies（W3）

> [!info] 问题链
> 方法论 = 结构化指导 plan / execute / deliver → 需求稳定选 Waterfall → 需求变化选 Agile → 高频发布选 DevOps → 选型也是 **organisational culture** 决策

### 为什么需要 methodology `★★`

- 组织工作框架；提高效率与沟通；管理风险、交付一致结果

**Term**：project management methodology / planned, executed, and delivered

---

### Waterfall `★★★`

> [!tip] 合规软件、建筑式项目——为什么用 Waterfall？考完还要会写缺点。

**中文**

- **Sequential, linear**：每阶段完成才进入下一阶段
- **Benefits**：结构清晰、文档完整、deliverables 明确；需求 **stable** 时好用
- **When to use**：fixed scope、少变更（compliance software, construction）
- **Disadvantages**：
  - **Inflexibility to changes**——回改代价高
  - **High risk for long projects**——交付前市场/技术可能已变
  - **Over-reliance on initial documentation**
  - **Limited stakeholder engagement during development**——易与最终期望错位

**例子**：NASA Space Shuttle software——需求极明确、安全关键、严格合规（DO-178B）；高可靠但 **years to complete**、成本巨大。

**Term**：sequential linear approach / well-defined deliverables / inflexibility to changes / over-reliance on initial documentation / limited stakeholder engagement

**Useful phrase**

- *Waterfall is a **sequential, linear approach** where each phase must be completed before the next begins.*
- *Once a phase is completed, it is **difficult and costly to go back** and make changes.*
- *Stakeholders are **less involved until final delivery**, which can lead to **misalignment with expectations**.*

<span style="color: #e67e22">*需求常变时，Agile 的迭代与反馈更合适。*</span>

---

### Agile `★★★`

**中文**

- **Iterative, flexible**；短周期 **sprints**；交付小步增量
- **Benefits**：适应变更；**stakeholder feedback early and often**；更快得到 working software
- **When to use**：需求 evolving（mobile apps 等）
- **例子**：Spotify Squad——周/日级发布，快速响应用户趋势

**课件**：Agile Manifesto 以图示呈现（Humphreys & Associates, 2019）——复习时对照课件图片。

**Term**：iterative approach / sprints / stakeholder feedback early and often / working software / evolving requirements

**Useful phrase**

- *Agile uses **short iterations** and **continuous feedback**.*
- *It **adapts to changing requirements** and encourages **stakeholder feedback early and often**.*
- ***Frequent testing and client reviews** enable **early detection of issues**.*（Practice Q4b）

---

### DevOps `★★`

**中文**：合并 **Dev + Ops**；**continuous integration, delivery, monitoring**；强调 automation、协作、快速发布。

- **Benefits**：faster release cycles；continuous testing/integration 提升质量；打破 dev/ops 壁垒
- **When to use**：高频发布、需 rapid deployment and scaling
- **例子**：Amazon——约每 11.7 秒部署一次

**Term**：continuous integration / continuous delivery / breaks silos / rapid releases

**Useful phrase**

- *DevOps combines development and operations for **continuous integration and delivery**.*

---

### Methodology vs organisational culture `★★`

| | Waterfall | Agile | DevOps |
|--|-----------|-------|--------|
| 文化 | command-and-control；重文档；层级清晰 | 协作；trust & psychological safety；透明 | shared responsibility；打破 silo；成熟文化 |

**Term**：command-and-control / psychological safety / shared responsibility / methodology choice is a cultural decision

**Useful phrase**

- *Methodology choice is a **cultural decision**, not just a technical one.*

---

### Methodology through professional lenses `★`

不同角色关注点：PM（control & reporting）、Sponsor（business case）、Developer（flexibility）、End User（usability & feedback）、Operations（reliability & automation）、Governance（documentation & traceability）、Change Manager（adoption & training）。

---

### Agile vs Waterfall（Practice Q4） `★★★`

| 小问 | 要点 |
|------|------|
| **a) 灵活性 & stakeholder** | Agile：短迭代 + continuous feedback，更 adapt to change；Waterfall：linear sequence |
| **b) 降风险** | Agile：frequent testing + client reviews → early detection，减少 late failures |
| **c) 转型挑战** | 团队不适应角色变化 → **training** and **clear communication**，建立 Agile mindsets |

详见 [[5990 复习笔记#Q4 Project Management Approaches：Agile vs Waterfall（8） `★★`]]。

## Part 3 — IT Lifecycle（W3）

### Business and IT silos `★★`

**中文**：传统开发 vs 运维 **worked in silos**——开发重功能/非功能需求；运维重成本、可靠性、安全、风险、可管理性。

**Term**：worked in silos / non-functional requirements / manageability

---

### Linear vs Continuous IT lifecycle `★★★`

> [!tip] 期末常考：linear 与 continuous 对比表 + HealthLink 为何转型。

| | **Linear（旧）** | **Continuous（新）** |
|--|----------------|----------------------|
| 流程 | Plan → Build → Deploy → End；**large, infrequent releases** | 持续增量改进；**frequent, small releases** |
| 方法论基础 | Waterfall；早期 **PMBOK**、**SDLC** | Agile、DevOps |
| 灵活性 | **Rigid**——阶段完成后难改 | **Highly adaptable**——持续迭代 |
| 风险 | 问题常 **detected late**（如测试阶段） | **Continuous testing** → early fix |
| 例子 | 传统开发、大型监管项目 | Agile、DevOps、云应用 |

**驱动因素**：digital transformation；customer expectations；cloud & automation；Agile & DevOps；data-driven decisions。

**Term**：linear approach / continuous lifecycle / cyclical and iterative / detected late in the process / continuous testing

**Useful phrase**

- *The continuous lifecycle is **cyclical and iterative**, allowing feedback and improvements.*
- *In a linear approach, changes are **difficult to implement once a phase is completed**.*
- ***Continuous testing** helps **identify and fix issues early**.*

---

### Continuous lifecycle 要素与指标 `★★★`

**定义**：recurring、iterative 管理 IT 系统；部署后不结束，持续 **monitoring, feedback, updates**。

**四阶段循环**（课件）：**Plan → Design/Build/Test → Deploy/Troubleshoot → Monitor** → 根据监控结果再进入 planning。

**Elements（展开）**

1. Planning aligned with business strategy  
2. Requirements, design, build/acquire, test  
3. Deploy（集成其他系统）、maintain service levels、troubleshooting、measure performance  
4. Monitor value → 触发新一轮 planning（cycle time 可长可短）

**成功指标**

| 指标 | 衡量什么 |
|------|----------|
| **Release frequency** | 多久部署一次新功能/修复 |
| **MTTR** | 平均修复故障时间 |
| **CSAT / NPS** | 用户满意度 |
| **Deployment success rate** | 无需 rollback 的部署比例 |

**Term**：mean time to repair (MTTR) / deployment success rate / release frequency / monitor the value to the organisation

**Useful phrase**

- *Instead of ending after deployment, IT work continues with **monitoring, feedback, and updates**.*
- ***Mean Time to Repair (MTTR)** measures the average time to resolve incidents.*

---

### IT system lifecycle phases（Practice Q3） `★★★`

> [!note] 下列阶段来自 **Practice PDF 答题要点**；W3 课件侧重 continuous 四阶段与 linear/SDLC 对比。写 MetroTrans 类题时用此八阶段。

| 阶段 | 目的（答题用） |
|------|----------------|
| planning | 定方向与范围 |
| analysis | 理解需求 |
| design | 方案设计 |
| development | 构建 |
| testing | 验证 |
| implementation | 上线 |
| operation | 运行维护 |
| retirement | 有序下线 |

**跳过 formal steps 的风险**（Practice）：undetected requirements errors；**technical debt** → cost overruns / failures。

**Regular lifecycle reviews**（Practice）：识别 **obsolescence**，及时更新，控制性能与成本。

**Term**：technical debt / undetected requirements errors / regular lifecycle reviews / obsolescence

**Useful phrase**

- *Phases include **planning, analysis, design, development, testing, implementation, operation, and retirement**—each ensuring requirements are met and risks are managed.*
- *Skipping phases leads to **technical debt**, causing **cost overruns and failures**.*
- *Regular reviews identify **obsolescence** and allow timely updates before major issues occur.*

详见 [[5990 复习笔记#Q3 IT Lifecycle and Project Phases（9） `★★`]]。

### Common pitfalls in continuous delivery `★★`

- **Over-automation without adequate testing**——缺陷进生产
- **Insufficient stakeholder communication** despite agile ceremonies
- **Security overlooked** in rapid deployments

**Term**：over-automation / insufficient stakeholder communication / security overlooked

## Part 4 — Enterprise Architecture（W3）

### EA 定义与价值 `★★★`

> [!tip] HealthLink：为何 Waterfall 不够，还要 continuous lifecycle + EA？

**中文**

- **Enterprise Architecture (EA)**：结构化框架，对齐 **business strategy, processes, information, technology**
- 目的：确保 IT 投资支持业务目标；提供 current & future systems 的 **blueprint**；**bridge the gap between vision and execution**

**为何重要**

- Strategic alignment；Efficiency（减重复与复杂度）；Agility；Risk management；Cost optimisation

**EA 如何支持 continuous delivery**

| 阶段 | EA 作用 |
|------|---------|
| Planning | roadmap for technology adoption and integration |
| Design & Build | standards 保证方案 fit broader ecosystem |
| Deploy & Monitor | interoperability and scalability |
| Feedback | EA evolves with new business needs |

**Term**：enterprise architecture / strategic alignment / blueprint / bridge the gap between vision and execution / interoperability / scalability

**Useful phrase**

- *EA provides a **blueprint** for current and future systems.*
- *The core idea is to **bridge the gap between vision and execution**.*
- *EA can help avoid project failures by ensuring **IT investments support business goals** and reducing duplication.*

---

### Zachman & TOGAF `★★`

**Zachman Framework**

- 按 stakeholder 视角与 **What, How, Where, Who, When, Why** 组织 EA
- 例（Student Portal）：数据、功能、网络、人员、时间、动机

**TOGAF**

- 设计、规划、实施、治理 EA 的方法论
- **ADM (Architecture Development Method)** 引导业务战略与 IT 对齐
- 实践组件：Business / Application / Data / Technology Architecture（数字转型案例：统一 enrolment、LMS+CRM+支付 API、集中数据库、云+SSO）

**Term**：Zachman Framework / TOGAF / Architecture Development Method (ADM) / business architecture / application architecture / data architecture / technology architecture

**Useful phrase**

- *TOGAF uses the **Architecture Development Method (ADM)** to align business strategy with IT systems.*

---

### HealthLink 案例 `★★`

**转型前问题**（课件）：business 与 IT **siloed**；更新延误；客户不满等待 bug fix 与 feature。

**转变**：Waterfall → **Continuous IT Lifecycle**，辅以 **TOGAF-based EA** → 更快发布、降本、满意度提升。

**答题角度**（课件思考题）

- 三个 major issues：silos、slow updates、poor customer satisfaction
- Continuous 优于 linear：需 **frequent fixes/features**、快速响应
- Agile alone vs DevOps：Agile 管迭代开发；**DevOps** 还打通 ops、自动化部署与监控——高频发布常需两者
- EA：roadmap、标准、集成新能力（如 AI analytics）时保证 **fit ecosystem**

**Term**：siloed teams / TOGAF-based enterprise architecture / smooth integration

## Part 5 — Information & Research（W5）

> [!info] 问题链
> 先 gather information → research 理解 → 用于 estimate time/cost/effort；信息须 **credible**，否则决策与估算都失真

### Information 与 DIKW `★★★`

> [!tip] MediCare 案例：原始患者数据如何变成 wisdom？

**Information（课件）**：可处理、组织、结构化以传达 knowledge / ideas / instructions 的 data 集合。

**DIKW**

| 层级 | 含义 | IT 中谁需要 |
|------|------|-------------|
| **Data** | 无上下文的原始事实 | 技术专家（日志、指标） |
| **Information** | 有意义的数据 | 各角色决策、沟通、报告的 **最低期望** |
| **Knowledge** | 经验与语境下的应用 | PM、顾问、BA、高级工程师 |
| **Wisdom** | 判断与决策能力 | 领导、架构师、高管 |

**Term**：DIKW hierarchy / raw facts / processed or organized data / evidence-based

**Useful phrase**

- *Information is **processed or organized data that has meaning**.*
- *Wisdom is the ability to make sound judgments using knowledge.*

**MediCare 答题思路（示例链）**：raw patient metrics (data) → structured reports (information) → clinical patterns (knowledge) → strategic staffing/resource decisions (wisdom)。

---

### Credible information & CRAAP `★★★`

**为何需要 credible information**

- Accuracy in decision-making；Trust and professional reputation；Risk reduction；Compliance and ethics；Efficient communication（共同 **source of truth**）

**Useful vs useless**

| Useful | Useless |
|--------|---------|
| Accurate, relevant, timely, complete, credible, understandable, actionable | Inaccurate, irrelevant, outdated, incomplete, redundant, unverifiable, ambiguous |

**Validity 层级**：High（医疗、安全、金融）/ Moderate（日常运维报告）/ Low（头脑风暴、趋势探索）

**CRAAP Test**

| 字母 | 问什么 |
|------|--------|
| **C**urrency | 是否最新 |
| **R**elevance | 是否相关、难度是否合适 |
| **A**uthority | 作者/机构是否可信 |
| **A**ccuracy | 是否有证据、是否 peer-reviewed |
| **P**urpose | 目的与偏见（inform / sell / persuade） |

**Term**：credible information / evidence-based / source of truth / CRAAP test / currency, relevance, authority, accuracy, purpose / validity

**Useful phrase**

- *Decisions are only as good as the **information behind them**.*
- *Credible information builds **trust** and supports **evidence-based decision making**.*
- *The **CRAAP test** evaluates **currency, relevance, authority, accuracy, and purpose**.*

---

### Primary / Secondary / Tertiary sources `★★`

| 类型 | 说明 | 例子 |
|------|------|------|
| **Primary** | 直接、原始、第一手 | 调研、访谈、公司记录与 logs |
| **Secondary** | 对 primary 的解释/总结 | 教材、文档、行业报告、新闻 |
| **Tertiary** | 汇总一二次来源 | 百科、词典、数据库 |

**Term**：primary sources / secondary sources / tertiary sources / firsthand evidence

---

### Research 基础 `★★`

**定义**：systematic 收集、分析、解释 information，回答问题或产生新知识。

**特征**：systematic；objective；**evidence based**；replicable；innovative

**类型**：Pure / Applied；Qualitative / Quantitative

**Primary vs Secondary research**（特征表见课件）：Primary 针对性强但费时费钱；Secondary 省时、视角广但依赖原来源可信度。

**何时用哪种**

- Primary：需要 specific、current、context-driven data（user testing, logs, surveys）
- Secondary：趋势、benchmark、背景（Gartner, IEEE papers）
- Tertiary：快速背景理解

**Limitations & bias**：小样本、设计差；过时、赞助偏见等。

**Term**：systematic process / evidence based / replicable / primary research / secondary research / feasibility study

**Useful phrase**

- *Research is a **systematic process** that is **evidence based** and **replicable**.*

**MediCare 案例**：Primary 例——用户调研、系统 logs；Secondary 例——IEEE 论文、行业 EHR 报告。

---

### Referencing `★`

- 目的：acknowledge authorship；support credibility；allow verification
- 风格：**APA7**（本课作业）、Harvard、IEEE

**Term**：plagiarism / APA7 / allow verification

## Part 6 — Estimation（W5）

### Estimation 的作用（Practice Q7） `★★★`

> [!tip] BrightEdge：需求在变、估算差 40%——先写 estimation 角色，再写不准原因与方法。

**中文**：在项目开始前预测 **effort, time, cost, resources**；用于软件开发、实施、测试、基础设施等。

**重要性**

- Project planning & scheduling  
- Budgeting & cost control  
- **Stakeholder expectations**（realistic deadlines，transparency）  
- Resource management  
- **Risk management**（提前发现 overrun）  
- Performance measurement（实际 vs 估计，改进未来预测）

**Term**：project planning and scheduling / budgeting and cost control / stakeholder expectations / contingency plans / performance measurement

**Useful phrase**

- *Estimation provides forecasts for **time, cost, and resources**, forming the foundation for **realistic schedules and budgets**.*
- *Accurate estimation ensures **client expectations, resource allocation, and profitability** are balanced despite evolving requirements.*（Practice Q7a）

---

### Effort → Resources → Time → Cost `★★★`

| 关系 | 含义 |
|------|------|
| **Effort** | 完成项目总工作量 |
| **Resources** | 人、技能、工具 |
| **Time (Duration)** | 日历时间；≠ effort（100h 可 1 人 2 周或 2 人 1 周） |
| **Cost** | (Effort × rate) + overheads |

**Key insight**：加人可能缩短时间但 **increases cost**；deadline 紧通常要更多资源；资源少则历时更长。

**Term**：person-hours / person-months / duration is not the same as effort / overheads

**Useful phrase**

- *Estimation is about understanding the relationship between **effort, resources, time, and cost**.*
- ***Effort and duration are not the same**: 100 hours of effort could take two weeks with one developer or one week with two.*

---

### 五步估算流程 `★★★`

1. **Determine SIZE** — scope、functionality、complexity（function points, story points, LOC, features）
2. **Determine EFFORT** — person-hours/days/months（COCOMO、agile 技术等）
3. **Decide RESOURCES** — 人力、技术、财务；按技能与可用性分配
4. **Calculate DURATION** — 给定资源与 effort 的日历时间
5. **Calculate COST** — (Effort × rate) + overheads

**Term**：function points / story points / work breakdown structure (WBS) / COCOMO

**Useful phrase**

- *The five steps are: determine **size**, **effort**, **resources**, **duration**, and **cost**.*

---

### 六种估算方法 `★★★`

| 方法 | 核心 | 何时用 | 局限/偏见（课件） |
|------|------|--------|-------------------|
| **Function Point Analysis (FPA)** | 按 inputs/outputs/interactions/data 计复杂度 | 需求清晰稳定；大型结构化系统 | 费时；依赖 estimator 解释；evaluator 不一致 |
| **Model-based (COCOMO)** | 数学模型 + 历史数据（如 KLOC） | 大型复杂项目 | **Garbage in, garbage out**；新技术不准 |
| **Expert judgement** | 有经验者凭类似项目判断 | 早期、需求不确定 | 主观乐观/悲观；**anchoring** |
| **Estimation by analogy** | 与历史项目比较并调整 | 有可比对的历史项目 | **false equivalence bias**；忽略差异 |
| **Task-based (WBS)** | 拆任务分别估再汇总 | 任务定义清楚 | 漏 integration；**anchoring** on parts |
| **Agile 技术** | Planning Poker、**Wideband Delphi**、velocity | 迭代开发 | 见下表 |

**COCOMO 例题要点**（40 KLOC HMS）：总 effort ≈ 110 person-months；团队并行后 duration ≈ 13 months——说明复杂度推高 effort，团队缩短 calendar time。

**Term**：function point analysis / constructive cost model (COCOMO) / expert judgement / estimation by analogy / work breakdown structure / planning poker / wideband delphi / velocity / anchoring bias / false equivalence bias

**Useful phrase**

- *Function Point Analysis estimates size by **inputs, outputs, user actions, and data**.*
- *COCOMO estimates effort based on **system size** and project complexity.*
- *Task-based estimation uses a **Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)** to estimate each task separately.*

### Agile vs Traditional estimation `★★`

| Aspect | Agile | Traditional |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| Approach | iterative, adaptive, **relative effort** | predictive, upfront size/effort/cost/schedule |
| Method | Planning Poker, Wideband Delphi, velocity | COCOMO, FPA, WBS |
| Focus | per story/feature | whole project upfront |
| Strengths | flexible, collaboration, re-estimation | clear budget/timeline when requirements stable |
| Limitations | 长期预测难；依赖 velocity；固定合同难 | 需求变更时不灵活；假设错则 **large variances** |

**Term**：relative effort / velocity tracking / large variances / fixed budgets and timelines

**Useful phrase**

- *Agile estimation is **iterative and adaptive**, while traditional estimation is **predictive** with detailed upfront planning.*

### 估算不准原因与方法选择（Practice Q7） `★★★`

**常见原因**（Practice + W5）

| 原因 | 影响 |
|------|------|
| **Unclear or changing requirements** | scope creep, rework → schedule/cost overruns |
| **Optimism bias / lack of historical data** | underestimate → missed deadlines, dissatisfaction |
| Expert judgement 偏见 | anchoring, subjective optimism |
| Analogy 偏见 | false equivalence |
| COCOMO | poor input data |

**需求不完整时推荐**（Practice）：**three-point estimation (PERT)** 或 **Delphi method**——用 best/worst/most-likely 或专家共识处理不确定性。

**W5 课件**：**Wideband Delphi** 列于 Agile estimation（与 Delphi 家族相关）；PERT 三字未在 W5 正文出现，以 Practice 为准作答。

**Term**：scope creep / optimism bias / three-point estimation / PERT / Delphi method / wideband delphi

**Useful phrase**

- *Unclear requirements cause **scope creep and rework**, leading to **schedule and cost overruns**.*
- ***Optimism bias** leads teams to **underestimate effort**, resulting in **missed deadlines**.*
- ***Three-point estimation (PERT)** or the **Delphi method** use expert judgment and probability to handle uncertainty.*（Practice Q7c）

详见 [[5990 复习笔记#Q7 Information and Estimation Accuracy（8） `★★`]]。
